Welcome to My Budding Bookself!

My name is Mattea and this is a website I created for readers and writers alike.
This is a place for creativity to blossom and grow. If you need a place to keep track
of the books you read or want to read document them here. If you need a place to write
your ideas down for stories or novels we have that too. Keep your thoughts here and let
them soak in the soil that is our site and they will be ready for you to come back and
water them with creativity where they will turn into beautiful gardens of ideas.
